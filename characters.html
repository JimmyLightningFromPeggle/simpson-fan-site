Meet the Key Characters of Springfield

← Back to the Homepage | Iconic Episodes →

Homer Simpson

The patriarch of the Simpson family, Homer is known for his simple-mindedness, love of donuts, and famous catchphrases like "D'oh!"  He works as a safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant.  While he often causes chaos, his deep, if often misplaced, love for his family is the core of his character.

Moe Szyslak

Moe is the owner and bartender of Moe's Tavern, a dingy establishment that serves as the local watering hole for Homer and his friends.  He is generally cynical and short-tempered, but occasionally shows glimpses of a caring, if lonely, person.  His constant prank phone calls from Bart are a classic running gag.

Ned Flanders

Ned Flanders is Homer's overly cheerful, devoutly religious, and annoyingly perfect next-door neighbor.  He is known for his signature greeting, "Hi-diddly-ho, neighborino!"  Despite Homer's consistent disapproval and often mean-spirited actions toward him, Ned remains unfailingly polite and generous.
