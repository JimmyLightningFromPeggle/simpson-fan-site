Last Website To Springfield

If I had to choose one show to watch for the rest of time, there's no doubt that it'd be The Simpsons.  The Simpsons has been my favorite show for years because of its clever blend of comedy, satire, and heart, especially during the early years of the show.  Not only has this show had an immense impact on my sense of humor, but it's defined a generation and its impact will not be forgotten.  I've seen every episode so many times that there are few episodes whose plot I don't know from the name alone.  There are so many iconic jokes, gags, and references that I find myself quoting whenever they're relevant, even when no one else seems to get it.
